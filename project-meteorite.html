Personal Project

NASA Meteorite & Bolide Report

This project involved compiling, normalizing, and analyzing planetary impact data from two distinct NASA Open Data Portal repositories: Meteorite Landings and Fireball/Bolide Reports. By building a data cleaning pipeline in Python and executing statistical and spatial analyses in Google Colab, I mapped the geographic, physical, and temporal trends of atmospheric cosmic events versus successful surface landings. Utilizing Matplotlib and Tableau, I engineered interactive dashboards and advanced visualizations to uncover key insights within the combined datasets.
